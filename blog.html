Today, many people all over the world use some form of computer every day. Having a general understanding of computers helps you function in today's world.


A ccomputer is an electronic device used to input, store, process and output data.


Functionalities of the computer.Any digital computer carries out five functions in gross terms:
1. Takes data as input.
2. Stores the data/instructions in its memory and use them when required.
3. Processes the data and converts it into useful information.
4. Generates the output.
5.Controls all the above four steps.
